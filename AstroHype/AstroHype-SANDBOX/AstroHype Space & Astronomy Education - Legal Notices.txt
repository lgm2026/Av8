1. ABOUT THIS APP
-----------------
AstroHype Space & Astronomy Education ("AstroHype," "the app") is a free,
offline educational study tool about space and astronomy. It is published and
maintained by its operator under the alias "openSpaceDB" (referred to here as

or have access to your name, your progress, or any usage data. Clearing your
browser data, or using the in-app reset, will permanently erase your saved

4. IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
-------------------------------
Observing the sky can be rewarding, but please read and follow these warnings:

  * NEVER look directly at the Sun with your unaided eyes, and NEVER point
    binoculars, a telescope, or a camera at the Sun, without proper, purpose-
    made, certified solar filters. Concentrated sunlight can cause instant and
    permanent blindness. Ordinary sunglasses, exposed film, smoked glass, and
    homemade filters are NOT safe. Solar viewing should be supervised by a
    knowledgeable adult using equipment designed and certified for the purpose.

  * Be careful observing outdoors at night. Choose safe, legal, well-known
    locations; bring a companion; tell someone your plans; watch your footing
    in the dark; and dress for the conditions. Minors should observe only with
    a responsible adult.

  * Respect private property, parks, and local laws and regulations regarding
    access, especially at night.

  * Use red light to preserve night vision, and be considerate of others by
    minimizing bright light and noise at shared observing sites.
