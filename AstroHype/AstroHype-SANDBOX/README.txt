AstroHype Space & Astronomy Education — Sandbox Preview
=======================================================

WHAT THIS IS
  A free, offline, single-file study app teaching space and astronomy to a
  curious all-ages audience: 501 bite-sized lessons across 8 tracks (Cosmic
  Foundations, Orbits & Gravity, The Solar System, Stars & Constellations,
  Galaxies & Deep Sky, Cosmology & Exotica, Missions & Spacecraft, and
  Skywatching/History/Careers), 1,428 quiz questions, a 26-game 8-bit arcade,
  XP/levels/streaks, badges, guided "expeditions," and a full reference library.

HOW TO RUN
  Just open "index.html" in any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge,
  Firefox). No server, no install, no internet connection required. On a phone
  you can "Add to Home Screen" to use it like an app.

NOTES
  • All progress is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). Clearing your
    browser data or using private/incognito mode will reset progress.
  • Each object lesson tries to load a reference photo online; offline, it shows
    a built-in illustration instead. Everything else works fully offline.
  • "index.html" and "AstroHype Space & Astronomy Education.html" are identical
    copies — use whichever name your setup prefers.

  See "AstroHype Space & Astronomy Education - Legal Notices.txt" for important
  educational-use, accuracy, and safety information (including: never look at
  the Sun through any optics without proper, certified solar filters).
