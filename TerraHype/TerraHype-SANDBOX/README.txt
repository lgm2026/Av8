TerraHype Nature Conservation Education — Sandbox Preview
========================================================

WHAT THIS IS
  A free, offline, single-file study app teaching North American nature and
  conservation to a 9–14 audience: 501 bite-sized lessons across 8 tracks
  (Foundations, Ecology, Flora, Fauna, Geology, Habitats, Conservation,
  Methods), 1,385 quiz questions, a 26-game 8-bit arcade, XP/levels/streaks,
  badges, guided "expeditions," and a full reference library.

HOW TO RUN
  Just open "index.html" in any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge,
  Firefox). No server, no install, no internet connection required. On a phone
  you can "Add to Home Screen" to use it like an app.

NOTES
  • All progress is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). Clearing your
    browser data or using private/incognito mode will reset progress.
  • Each species lesson tries to load a reference photo online; offline, it shows
    a built-in illustration instead. Everything else works fully offline.
  • "index.html" and "TerraHype Nature Conservation Education.html" are identical
    copies — use whichever name your setup prefers.
