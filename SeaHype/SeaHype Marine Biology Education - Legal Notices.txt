SeaHype Marine Biology Education is an independent educational study and

SAFETY
================================================================
Always follow local laws, regulations, and safety guidance for any beach,

================================================================
PRIVACY
================================================================
SeaHype runs entirely on your device. It does not require an account, and it
does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. Your name, learning progress,
flags, notes, and settings are stored only in your browser's local storage on
the device you use, under the storage key "seahype_marinebio_v1".

You can permanently erase all of this at any time from the Profile screen by
choosing "Reset progress." Clearing your browser's site data will also remove it.

Outbound links open third-party websites that have their own privacy practices;
those sites are outside our control.
