========================================================================

clear, representative photo of that EXACT species. The other 57 are concept
lessons that use scene illustrations (listed at the end — species photos there
are optional).

HOW TO MATCH LIKENESS: source by the SCIENTIFIC NAME / SEARCH TERM shown in the
middle column. For broad groups the term is the family/genus, so any clean
member of that group works.

FILE NAMING (if you later drop them into the bake step): save each photo as
<lesson-id>.jpg  (e.g.  fish-clownfish.jpg) — the id is the last column.
Prefer open-license sources (Wikimedia Commons, iNaturalist CC, NOAA public
domain). Landscape orientation, subject centered, no watermark.
